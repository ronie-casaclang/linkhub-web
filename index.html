Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Here are some common questions about LinkHub and how it can help you organize your online life.



What is LinkHub?

LinkHub is a free online tool where you can save, organize, and access all your favorite or important website links in one place. It's designed to simplify your digital life.

Is LinkHub free to use?

Yes, 100%! LinkHub is completely free to use — no subscriptions, no hidden fees. Just sign up and start saving your links.

Do I need to install anything?

Nope! LinkHub is a web-based platform. You don’t need to download or install anything. Just visit the site, log in, and you’re good to go on any device.

Can I use LinkHub on my phone?

Yes! LinkHub works smoothly on mobile browsers as well as on desktops and laptops. Your links are accessible wherever you are.

Is my data safe?

Yes. Your saved links are stored securely and are only visible to you. We respect your privacy and do not share your data with anyone.
